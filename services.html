OFC Laying & Execution

GN Management Services provides end-to-end solutions for laying fiber optic cables, including underground and overhead cabling, adhering to industry standards and best practices to ensure durable, high-performance installations.

Our Sr. Manager Rahul Kumar has successfully executed 50,000+ kilometers of OFC works throughout PAN India for different customers, covering NLD (National Long Distance) and Intracity routes.

End-to-end solutions for underground & overhead OFC. Adherence to industry standards for durable installations.

✅ Testing & Commissioning
Comprehensive testing of fiber optic systems to ensure optimal functionality. Quick commissioning to get networks operational.

Galvanized mobile towers placed on terraces of high-rise buildings for urban areas. Manufactured, supplied, and erected for leading mobile service providers and telecom infrastructure companies.
