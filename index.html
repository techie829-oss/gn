About GN Management Services

Delivering Integrated Solutions
Across India Since 2021

GN Management Services was established in 2021, headquartered at Faridabad, Haryana. We specialize in construction, Optical Fiber Cable (OFC) execution, Tower Upgradation, site interior works, and BRO projects. Our mission is to deliver quality work and complete customer satisfaction.

From planning and RF surveys to LOS and civil surveys, installation and commissioning of BTS/MW, drive testing and optimization — we provide comprehensive telecom solutions. We have grown to become one of India's leading integrated infrastructure companies.

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does GN Management Services offer?
+
GN Management Services offers 8 major service verticals: OFC Laying & Execution, Tower Erection & Maintenance, Civil Infrastructure, Industrial Shed & PEB Construction, Highway Safety Works, Solar Power Installation, Office Interior Solutions, and Design Engineering (TLVA/PLVA). We are a truly integrated infrastructure company.
Where does GN Management Services operate?
+
Our Head Office is at Plot No. 21, Railway Road, NIT-5, Faridabad 121001, Haryana. We also have a Branch Office at 375, Beicharana, Satwari, Jammu 180003. Our project execution spans PAN India, covering multiple telecom zones and states.
How many kilometers of OFC has GN Management Services executed?
+
Our Sr. Manager for OFC & FTTx, Rahul Kumar, has successfully executed around 50,000 kilometers of OFC works throughout PAN India for various customers. This includes NLD (National Long Distance) and Intracity OFC route construction.
Who are your major clients and project partners?
+
We currently collaborate with Praptham Tech Solution Pvt. Ltd. (Bangalore), Pace Digitek Ltd. (Bengaluru), and Melco India Pvt. Ltd. We are also actively working on the BSNL 4G Network Expansion Project, providing tower erection, painting, foundation, and solar installation services.
What quality standards does GN Management Services follow?
+
We use ISI certified materials (MS, ISMB, ISMC, H-Beams) and comply with BSNL and TEC specifications. We follow applicable Indian Standards and international standards where local standards are not available. We use anti-corrosive epoxy/enamel paints for long-life corrosion protection.
